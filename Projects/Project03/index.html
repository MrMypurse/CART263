A POEM LOVELY AS A TREE

YOU HAVE LEFT YOUR 9 TO 5 JOB IN THE CITY TO PERSUE THE SIMPLE FARM LIFE. ARRIVING AT YOUR NEW FARM, YOU FIND A SMALL SAPLING.

WATER IT, FERTILIZE IT AND CARE FOR IT LIKE YOU CARE FOR YOUR DEAREST FRIEND. IF YOU LOVE THE TREE, THE TREE WILL LOVE YOU BACK.

CLICK ANYWHERE OR SPEAK "START GAME" TO BEGIN.

TIPS:

- CLICK AND DRAG THE TOOLS TO CARE FOR YOUR TREE.

- IF YOU TOUCH YOUR TREE GENTLY, IT WILL SPEAK BACK TO YOU.

- WHISPERING SWEET WORDS TO THE TREE MIGHT HELP IT TO GROW FASTER.

TO MY DEAREST FRIEND:


I CHERISH THE TIME WE HAVE SPENT TOGETHER. BUT MY JOURNEY MUST COME TO AN END.

I APPRECIATE YOUR EFFORT TO CARE FOR ME. YOU ARE TRULY A LOVELY FRIEND. I WILL NEVER FORGET YOU. AND I WILL LEAVE YOU MY PRECIOUS FRUITS AS A GIFT.

MAY YOUR FUTURE BE BRIGHT. AND TAKE CARE.


YOUR FRIEND,

THE TREE
